§3 Pricing and Payments

  1. The parking fee (for a stay during opening hours 8:00–19:00) is:
    • 20 PLN — without reservation (first come, first served)
    • 25 PLN — with reservation (via Orzeł chatbot)
  2. The current price list is available at the car park entrance and on Google Maps.
  3. Payment is made to the car park staff upon entry or exit.
  4. Accepted payment methods: cash, BLIK, debit/credit card, Google Pay, Apple Pay.
  5. Failure to pay the parking fee will result in an additional charge of 100 PLN.
  6. If a vehicle has not left the car park by closing time (19:00), an additional charge of 50 PLN per each commenced hour will be applied as of 15 minutes after closing.

§4 Reservations

  1. Parking space reservations are accepted exclusively via the Orzeł chatbot (available at parkingsobieszewo.pl or through Facebook Messenger) or by phone at 784 828 748.
  2. The number of reserved spaces is limited to a maximum of 10 per day.
  3. A reservation is confirmed upon receiving a confirmation from the chatbot system.
  4. When making a reservation, the customer provides the vehicle registration plate number, which is binding — a vehicle with a different plate has no right to the reserved space.
  5. The reservation fee (25 PLN) is paid on-site after parking. No advance payment is required.
  6. A reservation may be cancelled at any time — via the Orzeł chatbot or by phone at 784 828 748.
  7. An uncancelled reservation combined with failure to appear may result in the blocking of future reservation privileges.
